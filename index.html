About AwakeAI Inc.
AwakeAI Inc (previously known as TrustCare), a McGill-based start-up, has developed a solution to improve care quality and address staff shortages at seniors’ homes and CHSLDs. It’s an AI-based system that analyses real-time video streams, detects unexpected activity changes, emergency cases, and triggers safety alerts. The technology ensures data privacy by eliminating the need for human observation of the video streams and personal data transmission.
An Automated Notification Service
AI-based monitoring system composed of a text-based information collecting module and a mobile phone app designed for receiving information
  • The information collecting module can locally process videos taken in the resident’s room and send concise textual information to family members and caregivers' mobile phones
  • Our system helps caregivers to respond in time to residents’ needs
